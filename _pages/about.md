## Biography

Hello! This is Wenjie Wei:blush:. I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in the [SCSE](https://www.scse.uestc.edu.cn/), [University of Electronic Science and Technology of China](https://en.uestc.edu.cn/), under the supervision of Prof. [Malu Zhang](https://sites.google.com/view/malu-zhang/home). Prior to this, I obtained my Bachelor’s degree from [Zhengzhou University](http://international.zzu.edu.cn/). My research interests broadly include neuromorphic computing and efficient deep learning, with a specific focus on *spiking neural networks* and *model compression* (e.g., *network binarization*, *quantization*, and *pruning*).
