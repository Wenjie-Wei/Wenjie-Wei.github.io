My name is Wenjie Wei. I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in the [School of Computer Science and Engineering](https://www.scse.uestc.edu.cn/), [University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC)](https://en.uestc.edu.cn/), under the supervision of Prof. [Malu Zhang](https://sites.google.com/view/malu-zhang/home). Prior to this, I obtained my Bachelor’s degree from [Zhengzhou University](http://international.zzu.edu.cn/) in 2017. My research interests broadly include neuromorphic computing and efficient deep learning, with a specific focus on spiking neural networks and deep model compression (e.g., network binarization, quantization, pruning, and distillation).
